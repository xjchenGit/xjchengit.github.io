Bio

I am currently a research assistant in Multimedia Information Retrieval Lab, National Taiwan University. I am pursuing my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSIE) from National Taiwan University (NTU), under the supervision of Prof. Hung-Yi Lee, and Prof. Jyh-Shing Roger Jang. Prior to attending NTU, I received my B.S. degree in CSIE from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology ( Taiwan Tech ) in 2020.

My research interests include but not limited to audio-visual learning, robustness, lightweight model.
