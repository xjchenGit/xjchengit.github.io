Bio

I am currently a research assistant in Multimedia Information Retrieval Lab (MIRLab), National Taiwan University (NTU). My research interests include but not limited to audio-visual learning, robustness, lightweight model.

I am pursuing my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSIE) from NTU, under the supervision of Prof. Hung-Yi Lee, and Prof. Jyh-Shing Roger Jang. Prior to attending NTU, I received my B.S. degree in CSIE from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST) in 2020.
