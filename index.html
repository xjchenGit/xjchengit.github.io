Biography

I am currently a research assistant at Multimedia Information Retrieval Lab of National Taiwan University. My research interests include but are not limited to deep learning, audio-visual learning, and speech processing.

I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSIE) from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2023, where I worked with Prof. Hung-Yi Lee and Prof. Jyh-Shing Roger Jang. Prior to attending NTU, I received my B.S. degree in CSIE from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (Taiwan Tech) in 2020.
